Steps to Cook Special Adobo Chicken

  1. Gather all ingredients:
    • 1 kg chicken (cut into serving pieces)
    • 1/2 cup soy sauce
    • 1/2 cup vinegar
    • 1 head garlic (minced)
    • 1 onion (sliced)
    • 2-3 bay leaves
    • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
    • 1-2 tbsp sugar (optional, for a touch of sweetness)
    • 2 tbsp cooking oil
    • 1 cup water
    • Salt to taste
  2. Prepare the chicken and aromatics:
    • Wash and drain chicken pieces.
    • Mince garlic and slice onion.
  3. Marinate the chicken:
    • In a bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, and half of the minced garlic.
    • Mix well and marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for best flavor).
  4. Sauté aromatics and brown the chicken:
    • Heat oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat.
    • Sauté onion until translucent, then add remaining garlic and cook until fragrant.
    • Add marinated chicken pieces (reserve marinade) and cook until lightly browned on all sides.
  5. Add liquids and spices:
    • Pour in reserved marinade, vinegar, and water.
    • Add bay leaves and peppercorns.
    • Do not stir after adding vinegar; let it boil uncovered for 2-3 minutes to cook off the acidity.
  6. Simmer and season:
    • Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for 20-30 minutes or until chicken is tender.
    • Add sugar if desired, and season with salt to taste.
    • Stir occasionally and let the sauce reduce to your preferred consistency.
  7. Finish and serve:
    • Remove bay leaves before serving.
    • Transfer to a serving dish and enjoy hot with steamed rice.
