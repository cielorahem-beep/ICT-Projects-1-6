Steps to Cook Special Sinigang

  1. Gather all ingredients: pork, sinigang mix, water, tomatoes, onions, radish, eggplant, string beans, okra, kangkong (water spinach), and green chili.
  2. Cut pork into serving pieces. Slice vegetables as needed.
  3. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add pork and simmer until tender. Skim off any scum that forms.
  4. Add onions and tomatoes. Continue to simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Add radish, eggplant, string beans, and okra. Cook until vegetables are half-tender.
  6. Pour in the sinigang mix and stir well. Add green chili for extra flavor.
  7. Add kangkong and simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Season with fish sauce or salt to taste. Serve hot with rice and enjoy your Special Sinigang!
